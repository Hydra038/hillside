BACKGROUND IMAGE SETUP
======================

Current Implementation:
- Using Unsplash image URL for immediate results
- Beautiful oak wood background with proper overlay

To Use Local Background Image:
1. Download a high-quality firewood background image (1920x1080 or larger)
2. Save it as: public/images/hero-background.jpg
3. Replace the style backgroundImage URL in HomeContent.tsx with:
   backgroundImage: `url('/images/hero-background.jpg')`

Recommended Background Images:
- Stacked firewood with natural lighting
- Wood pile with rustic barn or outdoor setting  
- Close-up of seasoned logs with visible grain
- Fireplace with burning logs (warm, inviting feel)

Image Requirements:
- Format: JPG or WebP for best performance
- Size: 1920x1080 or larger for crisp display
- File size: Keep under 500KB for fast loading
- Style: Natural lighting, warm tones, high contrast for text readability
