# Favicon Fix Instructions
========================

The favicon has been updated with a custom firewood design, but browsers cache favicons aggressively. Here's how to see the new icon:

## IMMEDIATE SOLUTIONS:
1. **Hard Refresh**: Press Ctrl+F5 (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)
2. **Clear Browser Cache**: 
   - Chrome: Settings > Privacy > Clear browsing data > Cached images
   - Firefox: Settings > Privacy > Clear Data > Cached content
3. **Private/Incognito Window**: Open the site in a private window
4. **Different Browser**: Try a different browser to see the new icon
